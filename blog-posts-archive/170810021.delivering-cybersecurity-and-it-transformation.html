Program Case Studies

1. GRC Transformation (Risk Reporting & Integrations)

Objective: Mature Ameren’s governance, risk, and compliance capabilities and automate a manual NIST CSF assessment process.

I led the multi-workstream program that delivered:

  • Full lifecycle implementation of the NIST CSF 2.0 process within ServiceNow IRM

  • A custom integration with the legacy ChangeGear system

  • Management of the Edgile ArC subscription to keep the regulatory control library continuously current

Key leadership actions:

  • Architectural stabilization: When internal processes shifted late in the cycle, I wrote the initial architecture vision myself and drove it through formal approval, avoiding long delays.

  • Critical API redesign: When we discovered the vendor lacked OAuth 2.0 support, I led a rapid engineering initiative to design a secure Basic Auth connector—approved under exception—preventing multi-month schedule impact.

  • Operational foundation: I authored the long-term GRC governance strategy to ensure the system’s sustainability well beyond go-live.

2. Privileged Access Management (PAM) Transformation

Objective: Establish enterprise-wide control of privileged accounts across IT and OT.

Taking leadership after the initial CyberArk tool selection, I architected and executed a multi-year transformation that included:

  • Building all CyberArk environments (Dev, Test, Prod, and protected OT)

  • Migrating thousands of privileged accounts

  • Leading five parallel workstreams across Accenture and CyberArk Professional Services

  • Navigating ~$5M in complex change orders

  • Deploying advanced features including Privileged Session Manager (PSM) and Vendor PAM (VPAM)

Outcome:

A mature, centralized PAM capability with automated password rotations, session recording, and audit-ready trails—dramatically reducing identity-based cyber risk.

3. Active Directory Modernization

Objective: Harden and modernize Ameren’s enterprise identity infrastructure.

Over three years I led the execution of the full AD Roadmap:

  • Upgraded all on-prem Domain Controllers with zero operational disruption

  • Expanded Silverfort MFA across critical assets

  • Integrated modern Azure AD features

  • Standardized cross-domain Group Policy administration

Outcome:

A resilient, modern, security-hardened identity platform capable of supporting Ameren’s long-term cyber strategy.

4. Adversary Simulation Automation (SafeBreach)

Objective: Replace slow, periodic manual penetration testing with continuous automated validation.

By deploying SafeBreach across IT and OT, we:

  • Identified configuration and control gaps in real time

  • Reduced O&M costs by automating many tests

  • Removed testing bottlenecks that had been slowing project timelines

  • Increased visibility into control performance across people, process, and technology

Outcome:

Continuous validation with real-time insights—transforming how Ameren tested and strengthened its security controls.
